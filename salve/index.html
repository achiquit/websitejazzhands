Just Salve

So, you're probably here because you came across some of my salve!
I make all of my salve myself from start to finish. I forage for resin, the active ingredient, being careful to not harm the trees that I harvest from. It's a wonderful way for me to connect with nature, get some quiet solitude, and I end up with quite a lot of salve! I then melt down the resin in oils and add the perfect amount of beeswax to create a nice salve to help speedily heal minor wounds (and make you smell like a tree to boot!)

I got into this because I was curious about making things from resin for some years, then I found myself with an extreme abundance of spare time surrounded by Spruce trees in the winter Norwegian countryside.
I used my time to forage for a ton of resin from the multitude of Spruce trees there and perfected my recipe for making salve!
Now, I want to pass on my simple, effective, homemade salve to people who might appreciate it :)

If you'd like to order some salve, reach out to me at my Contact Me page!
